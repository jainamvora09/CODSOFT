Agriculture cover a wide range of features that contribute to the cultivation of crops, raising livestock, and other farming practices.
Some key features of agriculture include:


Feature 1: Crop Cultivation

The cultivation of various crops such as grains, fruits, vegetables, oilseeds, fibers, and medicinal plants.

Feature 2: Livestock Rearing

Raising animals for various purposes, including meat, dairy, eggs, wool, and other by-products.

Feature 3: Irrigation

The application of water to land or soil to assist in the growth of crops by controlling moisture levels.

Feature 4: Mechanization:

The use of machinery and equipment like tractors, harvesters, plows, and irrigation systems to increase efficiency and productivity.

Feature 5: Agrochemical Use

Application of fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, and other chemicals to enhance crop production and protect against pests and diseases.

Feature 6: Sustainable Practices

Implementation of environmentally friendly and sustainable agricultural methods to conserve natural resources, reduce environmental impact, and maintain soil fertility.
